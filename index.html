RGB/CYM channel fusion for Harris Shutter composites

What is Harris Shutter?

Harris Shutter combines three photos by mapping each one to an independent channel. If there is motion between shots, it creates the characteristic color-shift effect. RGB works as additive light mixing and often looks strong on dark scenes; CYM is a subtractive-style mapping that can look striking on lighter backgrounds.
